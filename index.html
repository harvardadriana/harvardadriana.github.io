Can your personality be influenced by your blood type?

Blood type is to Japanese as horoscope is to westerns. Japanese take it very seriously a person’s blood type. It is believed that personality can be predicted according to the blood type of a person. This theory was popular in late 20’s and despite the little research done and inconclusive results, the theory was revived again during the 70’s. [1]

References to the blood type is used by dating agencies to job interviews. Products are developed according to the blood type: from bath salt to condoms. But does it really influence someone's personality?
